What is this tool?

You can insert your osu!mania scroll speed to it and get an accurate Etterna/SM CMod value.
The values have been checked frame by frame so it actually lines up with both games.


How accurate is this tool?

Here is a video comparison of different scroll speeds and receptor sizes between the games. The values were calculated using this calculator.

Huge thanks to Jole for this.

Keep in mind that this tool will only convert the raw velocity of the notes, so differences between receptor heights will not be accounted for.
